Specifications at a Glance

Binding
Spiral (coil) binding with punched edge; pages turn 360° and lay flat.
Punching and Design
Odd pages get punch on left side / Even pages get punch on right side for left side binding. View image View example in FAQ #5.
Sizes
8.5″×11″ • 5.5″×8.5″ • Portrait or Landscape (custom by request).
Minimum order
1 booklet.
Printing sides
Single-sided or Double-sided.
Inside paper options
20# uncoated (writable; no bleed accepted) • 24# uncoated (bright white) • 28# uncoated (Super 96) • 80# coated (gloss/silk) • 100# coated (gloss/silk).
Popular configs
See common paper + cover combos
Covers
100# Cover (gloss/silk) • 14pt Thick Cover (semi-gloss). UV coating available by custom request.
Plastic covers
Clear front vinyl and/or black back thick vinyl available.
Coil colors
Standard: Black. Special-order colors (blue, gold, green, white, etc.).
Tabs & dividers
Available by custom quote (sizes, positions, stocks vary).
Page counting
Count every side as a page, including printable covers.
Capacity / coil
Max coil Ø 30 mm. Capacity depends on paper; with copy paper, up to ~500 pages per book.
Bleed & safety
Choose “Bleed” (submit bleed-ready files) or “No Bleed” (0.25″ white margins). Keep text ≥ 0.5″ from punched edge.
Color options
Self-cover jobs: all pages Full Color or all pages B&W. If cover stock differs, cover bleed can be set independently.
Proof & turnaround
Turnaround varies by quantity/finishing—use the calculator for an estimate. Order before 11 AM and select “Faster” for expedited production.
File prep
PDF w/ embedded fonts • CMYK • 300 DPI images • 0.125″ bleed when required • single multi-page PDF including covers/blank pages; PDF page count must match selection.
Important: 20# uncoated does not accept bleed. For edge-to-edge designs, choose a heavier stock and select “Bleed.”
